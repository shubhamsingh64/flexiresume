The Problem

Hiring is broken
for small teams.

Enterprise tools are expensive and bloated. Manual processes are slow and chaotic. We built flexiResume because we lived this problem firsthand running our own IT services firm.

Enterprise Overkill & Cost Barrier

Platforms like LinkedIn, Workday, and Greenhouse cost $5k–$20k+ annually — priced for corporations with features irrelevant to smaller teams. Most startups simply can't afford them.

Fragmentation & Information Silos

Resumes scatter across email folders, spreadsheets, and Slack. Without a centralised process, tracking candidate status becomes a guessing game of disconnected threads.

The Efficiency Bottleneck

HR teams spend excessive hours on manual shortlisting and basic screening calls. Slow time-to-hire leads to missed deadlines and lost talent in competitive markets.

The Candidate "Black Hole"

Poor communication leaves candidates in the dark, destroying your employer brand. 73% of candidates never hear back — creating lasting negative sentiment about your company.
